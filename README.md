This project integrates robust data modeling on **SQL Server** with high-impact visual analytics in **Power BI** to monitor over **$127.91M in inventory value**, optimize product availability, and streamline supply chain decision-making.

## 🔍 Supply Chain Deep-Dive & Insights

### 1. Availability Portfolio Analysis
* 🏆 **Availability Leader:** **Osaka** achieved **78.81%** availability, driving the highest efficiency in inventory placement.
* ⚠️ **Availability Laggard:** **Munich** dropped to **77.71%**, signaling a critical need for safety stock modifications and replenishment audits.

### 2. High-Value Financial Distribution
* 💰 **Highest Exposure Factory:** **Pune** dominates the portfolio footprint, holding **$28,930,443.12** in raw inventory value and a pipeline of **31,040 units in-transit**.
* 📦 **Top Product Segments:** **Housing Systems** accounts for **$25.99M (20.32%)** of total inventory, representing the primary financial asset group.

### 3. Historical Tracking and Volume Peaks
* 📅 **Peak Volume Horizon:** Analytical parsing identified **2024** as the peak tracking year, accumulating **144K total units** in volume.
